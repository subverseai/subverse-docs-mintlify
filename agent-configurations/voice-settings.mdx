Configure the voice characteristics of your agent.

### TTS Model
Select the text-to-speech engine:
- **Elevenlabs [Eleven Flash V2.5]**: High quality, natural voices
- **OpenAI TTS**: Good quality, fast generation
- **Google TTS**: Wide language support

**Recommended:** Elevenlabs for most natural-sounding voices

### TTS Language
Select voice language:
- English (US)
- English (India)
- Hindi
- Multiple other languages

### Gender
Choose voice gender:
- **Male**: Deeper, authoritative tone
- **Female**: Often perceived as more friendly

**Selection Tips:**
- Match to your brand personality
- Consider target audience preferences
- Test both options with sample customers

### Voice
Select specific voice model:
- **Monika**: Professional, clear female voice
- **Leo**: Professional male voice
- **Riya**: Warm female voice
- **Asathi**: Energetic voice
- **Nikita**: Calm, soothing voice

**Test different voices** to find the best match for your use case.

### Speed
Control speaking rate (0-4 scale).

**Settings:**
- **0**: Very slow (for complex information)
- **1**: Slow (for elderly or non-native speakers)
- **2**: Normal (recommended for most cases)
- **3**: Fast (for time-sensitive calls)
- **4**: Very fast (use with caution)

**Recommended:** 2 for natural conversation pace

### Voice Stability (Expressiveness)
Controls emotional variation in voice (0.0-1.0).

<img
  style={{ borderRadius: '0.5rem' }}
  src="/images/agents/voice-settings-2.png"
/>

**Settings:**
- **0.0-0.3**: Very stable, monotone
- **0.4-0.6**: Balanced (recommended)
- **0.7-1.0**: Very expressive, emotional

**Recommended:** 0.5 for professional yet engaging tone

### Similarity Boost
Enhances voice consistency (0.0-1.0).

**Settings:**
- **0.0-0.5**: More variation
- **0.6-0.8**: Balanced (recommended)
- **0.9-1.0**: Maximum consistency

**Recommended:** 0.8 for consistent brand voice

### Voice Pitch
Adjust voice pitch (0-4 scale).

**Settings:**
- **0-1**: Lower pitch
- **2**: Normal pitch (recommended)
- **3-4**: Higher pitch

### Voice Volume
Control output volume (0-4 scale).

**Recommended:** 2 for standard volume
